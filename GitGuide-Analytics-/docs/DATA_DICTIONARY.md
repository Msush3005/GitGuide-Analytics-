# Data Dictionary

## Dataset Overview

This dataset contains customer transaction records collected from the CRM system.

**Last Updated:** 2025-05-21

**Maintained By:** Data Engineering Team

---

# Column to KPI Mapping

## Monthly Revenue

**Formula**

SUM(trnx_amt)

**Related Columns**

- trnx_amt
- purchase_date

**Why It Matters**

Measures total revenue generated.

**Update Frequency**

Daily

---

## Sales Velocity

**Formula**

COUNT(transactions) / days

**Related Columns**

- purchase_date

**Why It Matters**

Measures how quickly sales occur.

**Update Frequency**

Weekly

---

## Segment Revenue

**Formula**

SUM(trnx_amt) grouped by cust_segment

**Related Columns**

- trnx_amt
- cust_segment

**Why It Matters**

Identifies profitable customer segments.

**Update Frequency**

Monthly

---

## Churn Rate

**Formula**

SUM(flag_churn) / total_customers

**Related Columns**

- flag_churn
- customer_id

**Why It Matters**

Measures customer retention.

**Update Frequency**

Quarterly

---

# Columns

## customer_id

- **Type:** Integer
- **Business Meaning:** Unique customer identifier.
- **Example:** 12456
- **Null Handling:** Never null
- **Related KPI:** Customer Tracking
- **Updates:** Assigned during customer registration.

---

## trnx_amt

- **Type:** Float
- **Business Meaning:** Revenue generated by a transaction.
- **Example:** 150.99
- **Unit:** USD
- **Null Handling:** Investigate if null.
- **Related KPI:** Monthly Revenue, Average Transaction Value
- **Updates:** Recorded when payment succeeds.

---

## purchase_date

- **Type:** Datetime
- **Business Meaning:** Date of purchase.
- **Example:** 2025-01-15
- **Null Handling:** Never null
- **Related KPI:** Sales Velocity
- **Updates:** Automatically generated.

---

## cust_segment

- **Type:** String
- **Business Meaning:** Customer market category.
- **Valid Values:** B2B, B2C, SMB
- **Example:** B2B
- **Null Handling:** UNKNOWN if missing
- **Related KPI:** Segment Revenue
- **Updates:** Monthly.

---

## flag_churn

- **Type:** Integer
- **Business Meaning:** Indicates whether customer churned.
- **Values:** 0 = No, 1 = Yes
- **Example:** 0
- **Null Handling:** Never null
- **Related KPI:** Churn Rate
- **Updates:** Quarterly.

---

# Ambiguous Columns & Resolutions

## flag_churn

**Original Ambiguity**

Does it represent current churn or future churn?

**Resolved Meaning**

Customer churned within 90 days after the transaction.

**Business Interpretation**

Historical label used for churn prediction.

**Proposed Rename**

has_churned_90d

**Risk**

Incorrect churn calculations and unreliable ML models.

---

## cust_segment

**Original Ambiguity**

Does it represent customer type, product type, or geography?

**Resolved Meaning**

Customer market segment.

**Business Interpretation**

Used for pricing, marketing, and revenue analysis.

**Proposed Rename**

market_segment

**Risk**

Incorrect business reporting.
